## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades are immediate; downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

### What happens if I exceed my limits?
We'll notify you when you're approaching limits. You can upgrade anytime or purchase add-ons.

### Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees for any plan.

### Do you offer discounts?
- 20% discount for annual billing
- Non-profit discount: 25% off any plan
- Educational discount: 25% off any plan
- Multi-account discount: Contact sales

### Can I try before I buy?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan (no credit card required).

### What if I need help migrating?
Pro and Agency plans include migration assistance.
